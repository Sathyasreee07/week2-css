Kimi No Na Wa(Your Name)

Two strangers find themselves linked in a bizarre way. When a connection forms, will distance be the only thing to keep them apart?

Director: Makoto Shinkai

Writer:Makoto Shinkai, Clark Cheng

Stars: Ryunosuke Kamiki, Mone Kamishiraishi, Ryo Narita

Genre: Animation, Drama, Fantasy

Release Date: 2017-04-07

Language:Japanese

Synopsis

Mitsuha Miyamizu, a high school girl, yearns to live the life of a boy in the bustling city of Tokyo—a dream that stands in stark contrast to her present life in the countryside. Meanwhile in the city, Taki Tachibana lives a busy life as a high school student while juggling his part-time job and hopes for a future in architecture. One day, Mitsuha awakens in a room that is not her own and suddenly finds herself living the dream life in Tokyo—but in Taki's body! Elsewhere, Taki finds himself living Mitsuha's life in the humble countryside. In pursuit of an answer to this strange phenomenon, they begin to search for one another. Kimi no Na wa. revolves around Mitsuha and Taki's actions, which begin to have a dramatic impact on each other's lives, weaving them into a fabric held together by fate and circumstance.

Storyline

Mitsuha is the daughter of the mayor of a small mountain town. She's a straightforward high school girl who lives with her sister and her grandmother and has no qualms about letting it be known that she's uninterested in Shinto rituals or helping her father's electoral campaign. Instead she dreams of leaving the boring town and trying her luck in Tokyo. Taki is a high school boy in Tokyo who works part-time in an Italian restaurant and aspires to become an architect or an artist. Every night he has a strange dream where he becomes...a high school girl in a small mountain town.

Taki Tachibana

Taki is the male protagonist of the movie. He is a handsome Tokyo boy, who represents Mitsuha's dream of escape from the small town. The two find themselves switching bodies while they sleep and as this situation progresses the affection for one another grows.

Mitsuha Miyamizu

Mitsuha is the female protagonist of the movie. She is a young girl living in a small town called Itamari, and dreams of the big city of Tokyo. She spends her time in school or helping her grandmother with threading in a local shrine. Mitsuha suddenly finds herself switching bodies with a handsome boy from Tokyo, which she thinks at first as being a dream, but later has to get accustomed to the male body and Taki's daily life.

Miki Okudera

Miki is Taki's colleague from a restaurant he works part-time. She is beautiful and Taki has a crush on. While Mitsuha is in his body, she progresses with his relationship with Miki, even arranging dates for them. Miki soon realizes that Taki no longer likes her and senses another girl in his life.

Hitoha Miyamizu

Hitoha Miyamizu is Mitsuha's grandmother and head of the Miyamizu family. She takes care of the local shrine and is full of wisdom about religion and tradition.
